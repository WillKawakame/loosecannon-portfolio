Punch Princess

PunchPrincess follows Bell, a determined girl who dreams of becoming a professional boxer. In a setting of intense and challenging fights, she faces different opponents as she strives to prove her worth in the ring. The game conveys the pressure and excitement of each match, placing the player on this journey, where every fight is another step toward Bell’s dream.

Nosin Show

Nosin’ Show is a dynamic collection of minigames inspired by WarioWare Inc. In this game, you take on the role of Nosin, a charismatic yet unlucky mascot who, in a slapstick humor style, always ends up in trouble. Challenge yourself with fast-paced and funny games across a variety of themes and visual styles—one for each Nosin’ Show TV program—such as 1950s cartoons, pixel art video games, and minimalist vector design.

Samurai: Holo Town:

Samurai: Holo Town, our Android mobile game (tablets and smartphones), is a captivating pixel art endless jumper with a Neo Tokyo aesthetic, featuring a stunning visual style centered around its CRT shader. Developed with the intention of evoking old monitors, the game invites you to embark on an addictive adventure as Holo, a sentient byte-shaped hologram who protects the great vertical city by jumping from hologram to hologram.

About us

LooseCannon Game Studio is an independent studio focused on creating unique and memorable experiences. Our games explore different visual styles and mechanics, always aiming for a fun result. We are currently a team of two Brazilian developers, working together to build our projects.
